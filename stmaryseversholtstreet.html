St Mary's Somers Town

Introduction

Although ULCC has no fixed place to worship and sing, St Mary's Somers Town in Eversholt Street, London is our regular rehearsal venue.

Rehearsals are Friday evenings in term time and are published on the calendar.

Where is it?

St Mary's Somers Town is at the intersection of Eversholt Street and Aldenham Street, roughly midway between Euston station (National Rail and Underground) and Mornington Crescent Underground station.
