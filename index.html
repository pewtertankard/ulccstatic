The University of London Church Choir (ULCC) is a choir of graduates and students who live and work in and around London. We sing church services at places in and around London where there is either no choir, or where the regular choir is unable to perform. We aim to sing a service or concert every three to four weeks during the University term.

News

As you are no doubt aware, the ongoing Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic has resulted in a cessation of public services of worship and this, of course, includes cancelling all our near-to planned events. We will soon be able to confirm if we can continue with our summer tour in August at Wells Cathedral and, hopefully, our planned weekend in Bradford Cathedral this July. For more details, see here.

Secondly, full rehearsals are, of course, not able to occur right now. Our planned next full rehearsal is therefore the beginning of the next academic year, Friday, the 25th of September 2020. However, rehearsals have not stopped. They've just gone online! If you'd like to know more or were wondering about joining us, just get in touch, details are here.
