Welcome

The University of London Church Choir (ULCC) is a choir of graduates and students who live and work in and around London. We sing church services at places in and around London where there is either no choir, or where the regular choir is unable to perform. We aim to sing a service or concert every three to four weeks during the University term.

We’re a friendly bunch and welcome new singers, of any age, whether experienced or not.

Sing joyfully!
We sing church services in and around London where there’s no regular choir or where the regular choir is unable to perform.
You don’t have to be a student or associated with the University of London at all. Don’t be confused by our name. If you love singing, we’d love to meet you.
We sing at several services every term, including usually one service at Southwark Cathedral.
Recent tours have been to Exeter, Durham and Liverpool Cathedrals and to St Thomas’ Fifth Avenue, New York.
We’re a group of amateur singers who are passionate about singing English Choral music in its proper liturgical setting.
